📘 How BizPlanner Works

BizPlanner helps aspiring entrepreneurs understand how to allocate their investment wisely based on the city and business type. We use real-world approximations to generate your budget breakdown and estimate breakeven sales needed for success.

📊 Budget Categories

  • 🏢 Rent: Cost of commercial property per sq ft.
  • 💼 Salaries: Staff payments (chefs, managers, helpers).
  • 🛠 Equipment: Furniture, tools, tech & setup items.
  • 📢 Marketing: Social media, banners, local ads, promotions.
  • 📄 Legal: Licenses like FSSAI/GST, consultancy.
  • ⚙ Miscellaneous: Maintenance, software, packaging, internet, etc.

📍 Smart City-Based Planning

The breakdown adapts to your selected city. For example, a 5-star hotel might perform well in Ahmedabad but may not suit Kapadwanj. Our AI provides an insight on the business potential based on city-business compatibility.
